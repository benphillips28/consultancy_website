Our Services & Experience

1. Evidence Synthesis

We are experienced in collating evidence (on a wide variety of topics) to a rigorous scientific standard.

Our experience includes reviewing evidence for...

  • Enhancing road verges for pollinators (with the conservation charity Buglife), and for other environmental and social benefits.
  • Environmental links between forests and oceans for The Woodland Trust.
  • Microplastic pollution and marine megafauna entanglements. EMILY TO EDIT + PROVIDE LINKS

2. Data Services & Geographical Information Systems (GIS)

We can assist with managing, visualising and analysing ecological and social data, including spatial mapping and analysis and using large, unwieldy datasets.

Our experience includes...

  • Modelling road pollution across Great Britain.
  • Remote assessment of road verge habitats using Google Earth and Google Street View.
  • Comparing light pollution levels across UK local authorities and habitats.

3. Social Science Approaches

We are experienced in researching people's attitudes, values and behaviours - particularly how people engage with nature - using surveys, interviews and digital data sources. This information can help to shape more effective and inclusive strategies in nature conservation and beyond.

Our experience includes...

  • Questionnaire survey design, including developing a major national survey of 18,000 adults across England with Natural England, an online survey campaign with The Wildlife Trusts as part of their 30 Days Wild campaign, and an online survey of public attitudes and behaviours relating to pollinators.
  • Analysis of large-scale survey, social media and digital data sources relating to people-nature relations with Natural England.
  • Conducting stakeholder interviews and focus groups to guide a conservation strategy for hazel dormouse.

4. General Scientific Consultancy Services

We are also able to provide organisations with general scientific consultancy services, including grant writing, programme development, and practical management guidance relating to both the terrestrial and marine environments.

Our experience includes...

  • Grant writing - We have a wealth of experience in writing and reviewing grants, including EMILY's AWARDED GRANTS AND EMILY'S GRANT PANEL FOR DEFRA.
  • Programme development - We have been responsible for developing, managing and delivering research programmes, so can offer support in this area.
  • Habitat / land management - Particularly relating to road verges, public spaces, gardens, farmland and pollinators.
  • Marine environment - Particularly relating to marine megafauna, fishing and microplastics. (EMILY TO EDIT)
